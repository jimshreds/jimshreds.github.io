Beach InSight

Stop checking four apps before you hit the beach.

Beach InSight combines air temperature, water conditions, tides, wave forecasts, and UV index into a single scrollable 24-hour timeline — so you can see at a glance exactly when conditions are perfect.

One view. Everything you need.

Unlike weather apps that ignore the ocean and surf apps that ignore the sky, Beach Day was built around the full beach experience. Every hour of your day, laid out in one place..

Included

  • Hourly air temperature (°F or °C)
  • Wind speed, direction, and gusts with compass display
  • Cloud cover with visual sky tinting — blue to grey at a glance
  • Live water temperature from NOAA ocean buoys
  • Sunrise & sunset times
  • Moon phase (8 phases)
  • East Coast bug warnings — know before you go when westerly winds push biting insects to shore
  • Home screen widget (small & medium)
  • Offline mode — full 24-hour data cached for when you lose signal at the beach

Premium

  • Tide predictions — exact high/low tide times and heights from official NOAA stations
  • Wave height, period, and direction from live marine forecasts
  • Hourly UV index with severity ratings (Low → Extreme)
  • Share your beach report — export and send the day to friends & family
